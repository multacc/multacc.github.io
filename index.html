Universal contacts and messaging

One place for all your contact needs

Multacc is a contact management tool that allows you to easily keep track of people across various platforms. You can create a profile with your own contact information and share it with a QR code or link. You can also send messages on various platforms from within the app (currently supports GroupMe, with Discord coming soon).
